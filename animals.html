    Wild Animals

    These are mammals,birds,fish,or other creature of a wild nature endowed with sensation and the power of voluntary motion. Examples of wild animals are:

    • Lions
    • Hyena
    • Elephant
    • Zebra
    1. Lions
      2.

      The lion is a large cat of the genus panthera. It has a muscular, broad-chested body, short, rounded head,round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

    2. Hyenas
      3.

      There are three hyena species-spotted,brown and spotted hyenas are the largest of the three. They are fairly large in build and have relatively short torsos with lower hindquarters, and slopping backs. They have excellent night-time vision and hearing. Hyenas have long forelegs and a powerful neck and shoulders for dismembering and carrying prey.

    3. Cheetah
      4.

      A cheetah is a large cat and native to Africa and central Iran. It is the fastest land animal, estimated to be capable of running at 80 to 128km/h
